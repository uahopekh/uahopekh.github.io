HOPE — Home Of Paws Everywhere

Who we are

HOPE (Home Of Paws Everywhere) is a small independent volunteer initiative based in Kharkiv, Ukraine.

We rescue abandoned and homeless animals from the streets, yards and dangerous areas of the city. Many of them are injured, sick, frightened or simply left behind.

We take responsibility for each animal we save — from the first day on the street to a safe home.

Core principles
Care until adoption
No salaries
Transparency
Focus on treatment

What we do

Full-cycle care for homeless animals

  • Medical treatment (injuries, infections, recovery)
  • Sterilization / neutering
  • Daily care and rehabilitation
  • Finding families and permanent homes

We do not abandon animals halfway.

Every animal stays with us until it is safe, healthy and adopted.

Treatment Vet care and medicines
Sterilization Stop suffering & overpopulation
Adoption Safe home, forever

Why your support matters

Kharkiv is a frontline city.

The number of abandoned animals grows every month, while resources are extremely limited.

Without financial support: animals cannot be treated, sterilization cannot continue, rescued animals have nowhere to go.

Your help directly saves lives.

Without support
  • Animals cannot be treated
  • Sterilization cannot continue
  • Rescued animals have nowhere to go

How your donation is used

Every euro goes directly to animals

  • Veterinary clinics and medicines
  • Sterilization costs
  • Food and basic supplies
  • Emergency treatment

We work without salaries — only real help, real animals, real results.
