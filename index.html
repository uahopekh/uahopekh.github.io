Who we are

HOPE (Home Of Paws Everywhere) is a small independent volunteer initiative based in Kharkiv, Ukraine.

We rescue abandoned and homeless animals from the streets, yards and dangerous areas of the city. Many of them are injured, sick, frightened or simply left behind.

We take responsibility for each animal we save ‚Äî from the first day on the street to a safe home.

Core principles

What we do

Full-cycle care for homeless animals

We do not abandon animals halfway.
Every animal stays with us until it is safe, healthy and adopted.

Why your support matters

Kharkiv is a frontline city.

The number of abandoned animals grows every month, while resources are extremely limited.

Without financial support: animals cannot be treated, sterilization cannot continue, rescued animals have nowhere to go.

Your help directly saves lives.
